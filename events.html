Football Events - March 2025

  • Carabao Cup Final: Liverpool vs. Newcastle United

    Date: Sunday, March 16, 2025

    Time: 4:30 pm GMT (9:45 pm NPT)

    Venue: Wembley Stadium, London

    Details: Liverpool, the defending champions, aim for their 11th League Cup title, while Newcastle seeks their first domestic trophy since 1955. Due to injuries, Liverpool will miss key players Trent Alexander-Arnold and Joe Gomez.

  • UEFA Nations League Quarterfinals: Germany vs. Italy

    Date: March 20, 2025 (San Siro, Milan) and March 23, 2025 (Signal Iduna Park, Dortmund)

    Details: Germany faces Italy in the UEFA Nations League quarterfinals. Germany will miss several key players due to injuries and illnesses, including Leon Goretzka, Joshua Kimmich, Jamal Musiala, and Leroy Sané.

  • CONCACAF Nations League Finals

    Date: March 20 to 23, 2025

    Venue: SoFi Stadium, Inglewood, California

    Details: The national teams of the United States, Mexico, Canada, and Costa Rica will compete in the CONCACAF Nations League Finals. The U.S. is the three-time defending champion.

  • U-20 Copa Libertadores

    Date: March 1 to 16, 2025

    Venue: San Lorenzo and Ypané, Paraguay

    Details: South America's premier under-20 club football tournament. Flamengo are the defending champions.
